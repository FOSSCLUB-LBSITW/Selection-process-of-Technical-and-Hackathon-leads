What is FOSS

Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) is a type of software that ensures users have the freedom to use, study, modify, and distribute the software. Unlike proprietary software, which restricts these freedoms, FOSS promotes collaboration and sharing, fostering a community-driven approach to software development.

FOSS LBSITW

At FOSS LBSITW, we are dedicated to promoting the use of free and open-source software. Our mission is to educate, collaborate, and contribute to the FOSS community. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just getting started, our club offers a welcoming environment to learn, share, and grow.

Goals

To promote the spirit of hacking and tinkering.

To build quality FOSS for public good.

To evangelise the use of FOSS in different sectors.
